Frequently Aked Questions

How long does is the shipping time?

The shipping time depends on the availability of the product in your local deposit. If the product is in stock, it should arrive in 2-3 workdays, otherwise a special request for your product will be placed and it should arrive from the main deposit in about 5-6 workdays.

Will COVID-19 pandemic affect my order?

Usually, there should be no delay to the shipping caused by the pandemic that we are facing. Stay pacient and healthy!

Can I return the product for a refund if I don't like it?

Of course! Our company ensures a 14-days trial period for your products and during that time you can request a refund.

If I am not satisfied with a product that I have purchased, how can I get a refund?

If a product is defectuous or if you don't like it you can send an email (go to the Send an email section in the Contact Us page) in which you specify that you want a refund and you will be shortly contacted by one of our staff members that will help you with your refund request.

Can I apply more than one promo code on the same order?

Unfortunately, it's possible to apply only one promo code on each order that you place.
